Nanjing, abbreviated as Ning, is the capital of Jiangsu Province, a sub-provincial city, the political, economic, scientific, educational and cultural center of the province, the first batch of Chinese historical and cultural cities and the national key scenic tourist cities identified by the Chinese State Council. Nanjing is located at the intersection of China's open coastal zone and the Yangtze River basin development zone, and is an important regional center city in the core economic zone of the Yangtze River Delta, as well as a national important comprehensive transportation hub and communication hub city.

Nanjing is one of the four ancient capitals of China, known as the "ancient capital of the six dynasties" and the "capital of the ten dynasties", with a long history, a rich cultural heritage, many different kinds of relics and many famous people in history, and is one of the important birthplaces of Chinese civilization and the meeting place of the Yellow River civilization and the Yangtze River civilization.

Nanjing is an important transportation and communication hub in East China, and has established an all-round, three-dimensional, high-capacity transportation network with a full range of five modes of transportation: rail, road, water, air and pipeline, and the Nanjing South Station of the high-speed railway is the largest railroad hub center in Asia with a modern communication system.

Nanjing is an important comprehensive industrial production base in China. After years of development, Nanjing has formed an industrial pattern with electronic information, petrochemical, automobile manufacturing and steel as the pillars, supported by new industries such as software and service outsourcing, smart grid, wind power and photovoltaic, and rail transportation, and the coordinated development of advanced manufacturing and modern services. Nanjing is ranked 5th among the "Top 50" Chinese cities in terms of comprehensive strength and is internationally regarded as one of the most promising cities in the Pacific Rim region of Asia in the 21st century.

Nanjing is a modern city full of charm, vitality and potential. It has been awarded as one of the first historical and cultural cities in China, a national civilized city, a national sanitary city, a national garden city, a UN Habitat Special Honor Award, and the first software city in China. Nanjing has been nationally recognized as the only pilot city for comprehensive reform of the science and technology system, a pilot city for innovative cities, and a pilot city for the integration of three networks.
